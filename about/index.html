Our Story

Technology With Purpose

Tinaht was founded with a simple belief: every business deserves access to enterprise-grade technology solutions, regardless of size. Too many companies struggle with outdated infrastructure, slow workflows, and security vulnerabilities — not because solutions don't exist, but because they seem out of reach.

We bridge that gap. Our team of engineers, developers, and consultants bring decades of combined experience in networking, cloud infrastructure, AI automation, and cybersecurity. We don't just implement technology — we partner with you to understand your challenges and build solutions that grow with your business.

From our first network deployment to our latest AI automation project, every engagement is guided by the same principles: reliability, security, and measurable results.
